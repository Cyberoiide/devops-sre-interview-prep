# Career

Study material focused on the non-code side of landing and growing in a
DevOps/SRE role: what to master, how to demonstrate it, and how to prepare for
interviews.

## Modules

| Module | What it covers |
|--------|----------------|
| [top-5-topics-2026](./top-5-topics-2026/) | The five technical topics that dominate DevOps/SRE job descriptions in 2026, with a self-assessment, a detailed study roadmap, and broader career follow-ups. |

## How to use this section

1. Start with [top-5-topics-2026/README.md](./top-5-topics-2026/README.md) to
   understand *why* these five topics matter and how they were chosen.
2. Run the self-assessment in
   [top-5-topics-2026/exercise.md](./top-5-topics-2026/exercise.md) to find your
   weakest topic.
3. Work the roadmap in
   [top-5-topics-2026/solution.md](./top-5-topics-2026/solution.md) for the
   topics you rated lowest.
4. Read [top-5-topics-2026/deeper.md](./top-5-topics-2026/deeper.md) for
   cross-cutting career advice (cloud choice, portfolio, interview formats).
